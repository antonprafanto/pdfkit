## Short Descriptions

### One-liner
> PDF Kit is a free, open-source desktop application for managing PDF documents offline.

### Elevator Pitch
> PDF Kit gives you all the PDF tools you need in one app - split, merge, compress, encrypt, annotate, and even chat with AI. Everything runs locally on your computer, so your documents stay private. It's free, open-source, and works on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

---

## Feature List (Bullet Points)

**Core Features:**
- Split PDF by page range, every N pages, or individual pages
- Merge multiple PDFs with drag-and-drop reordering
- Compress PDFs with adjustable quality presets
- Encrypt with AES-256 password protection
- Edit document metadata (title, author, subject)

**Conversion:**
- PDF to Images (PNG, JPEG)
- Images to PDF
- Office to PDF (with LibreOffice)

**Advanced:**
- Side-by-side document comparison
- Sticky note annotations
- Form filling and editing
- OCR for scanned documents

**AI Features:**
- Chat with PDF using RAG
- Document summarization
- Semantic search
- Supports OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini

**Organization:**
- Favorites (starred files)
- Collections (virtual folders)
- Tags (color-coded labels)
- Advanced search with filters
